Who are we?
Developer Student Clubs are completely non-profitable university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
DSC RSU was established in August, 2020 at Raksha Shakti University campus for School of Information Technology and Cyber Security. The official website under Google Developer’s DSC can be found here and the website under the Club can be found here
What is DSC RSU’s Vision?
The DSC RSU aims to provide a 360 degree view for students to polish their skills starting from communication to projects to applying to their dream companies. Main objective of the DSC RSU is to provide every possible resource be it technical or physical to all the interested students who join the Club as a Core Team and a Member of Club to excel in their careers by providing them practical sessions and seminars under Google Developer Experts and well known mentors. It plans to prepare students for careers in private and public industries.
Moreover, for the newbie students who want to deep dive into their interested field will also have a perfect environment to overcome their problems with the help of this community.
